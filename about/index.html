About

Computations is a public R&D lab that works at the intersections of digital humanities, social sciences and computer science.

We conceptualise, operationalise and develop IT solutions in Social Sciences and Humanities and Cultural Heritage, both for our original projects and in cooperation with large digital infrastructure providers.

If you are a:

- public digital infrastructure provider in the SSH and Cultural Heritage domains,

- IT company looking for collaboration,

- GLAM institution,

- SSH researcher,

contact us and let's work together!

We are particularly passionate about:

- automation and digitisation of processes,

- data and metadata standardisation,

- data enrichment, Linked Open Data and Semantic Web,

- data-driven research,

- implementing IT solutions (discovery services, bibliographies),

- providing recommendations and guidelines,

- courses and education.
